Frequently Asked Questions

What is the adoption fee?

The adoption fee varies by pet, typically covering vaccinations and spaying/neutering.

Do I need to bring anything to adopt a pet?

Please bring a valid ID and proof of residence. If adopting a dog, it’s helpful to bring any other pets to meet the new addition.
